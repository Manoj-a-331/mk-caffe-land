French Fries

French fries, also known as "frites" in French, are a popular and beloved dish enjoyed worldwide. They consist of potato sticks that are deep-fried until golden brown, resulting in a crispy exterior and a soft, fluffy interior. French fries are commonly served as a side dish to accompany burgers, sandwiches, fried chicken, and various other main courses.

burgers

"Burgers" typically refers to a popular food item consisting of a cooked patty of ground meat (usually beef), placed inside a sliced bun or bread roll. Burgers are often served with various condiments, toppings, and accompaniments, such as lettuce, tomato, onions, cheese, pickles, ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise. The origins of the modern hamburger are somewhat debated, but it is widely associated with the United States.
